Research themes

Dispersal, coexistence, and spatial theory

I use comparative modeling to understand how assumptions and model structure change predicted outcomes for coexistence and dispersal in heterogeneous landscapes, integrating deterministic and stochastic approaches.

Invasion dynamics and biocontrol

Through collaborations with USGS and USDA programs in the Greater Everglades, I apply theory-grounded models to evaluate invasion trajectories, management scenarios, and biocontrol effectiveness for restoration planning.
